Intro

Hi there! My name is James Gadea, and I work as a Tech Scout for Booz Allen Hamilton, you can check out my work here.

I love connecting people, learning about technology, living in San Francisco, and most importantly rejoicing in Jesus.

Work

I graduated from Georgetown University's School of Foreign Service in 2016 with a degree in International History and Jewish Civilization. Throughout my time in college, I was simultanteously drawn to the world of startups and the world of governance, international relations, and history. My childhood consisted of an equal diet of Popular Science magazines and atlases.

Since graduating from Georgetown, I've worked at the Israeli Economic Mission in San Francisco where I was the lead for the automotive sector, connecting Silicon Valley investors with Israeli entrepreneurs. After close to 2 years working for the Israeli Economic Mission, I spent about a year in southern Taiwan doing a missions internship at a small Baptist church. After coming back from an incredible experience in Taiwan, I began working as a Tech Scout with Booz Allen Hamilton in San Francisco.
